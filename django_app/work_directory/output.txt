		<p>The order of the cards matters. Thus, Scythe+Anchor and Anchor+Scythe have different meanings. </p>
<p>Look at the card themselves&#8230;</p>
<p>Scythe+Anchor: The Scythe is about quick action, letting go, fast decisions.  What comes after &#8211; the Anchor &#8211; safety, long term, goals&#8230; If you put them together you have quick action brings safety, letting go is for the long term (such as, permanent removal of something from your life), a fast decision about your goals, etc.</p>
<p>Anchor+Scythe could mean a variety of things depending on the question asked. But do the same as with Scythe+Anchor, simple switching the order. Some possible meanings include sudden changes, goals abruptly ending, or long-term security ending. </p>
